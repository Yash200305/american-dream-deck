## 🧠 Design Rationale & Product Thinking
[cite_start]Unlike a traditional website or a linear slide deck, this tool is built as an interactive "Digideck-style" experience[cite: 21, 75, 99].
* [cite_start]**Non-Linear Navigation**: The viewer controls their own journey through the content, allowing sales reps to pivot instantly based on prospect interest during live calls[cite: 24, 65].
* [cite_start]**Video-First Storytelling**: Video is the primary narrative medium, not mere decoration, creating immediate emotional buy-in within the first 10 seconds[cite: 24, 32, 65].
* [cite_start]**Luxury UI Standards**: Drawing inspiration from brands like Apple and Tesla, the UI uses minimal "chrome" and maximum visual impact[cite: 65, 85, 86].
* [cite_start]**Action-Oriented Design**: Every section is designed to move the prospect closer to a specific business action: signing a lease, committing to a sponsorship, or booking a venue[cite: 20, 37].
